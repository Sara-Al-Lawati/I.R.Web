    Certificate IV in Surveying

    Diploma of Surveying

    A course that helps you enhance your practical surveying skills, design basic engineering structures, and carry out surveying calculations

    Associate Degree of Surveying

    A stepping stone into a Bachelor of Surveying Technology or a Bachelor of Surveying (Honours)
