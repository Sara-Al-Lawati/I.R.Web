To become a land surveyor in Australia, you typically need:

  • A bachelor's degree in surveying, geomatics, or a related field
  • Additional training such as a Certificate IV, Diploma, or Associate Degree in Surveying to build technical skills
  • Practical experience and passing board examinations for registration as a licensed surveyor
